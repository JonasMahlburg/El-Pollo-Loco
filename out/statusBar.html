Class: statusBar

statusBar()

The statusBar class represents a status bar for displaying health or similar metrics in the game, extending the drawableObject class.

Constructor

new statusBar()

Constructs a new statusBar instance. Loads the images for different percentage levels, sets the position and dimensions of the status bar, and initializes it with a percentage of 100.
