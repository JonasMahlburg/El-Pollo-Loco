Class: salsaBar

salsaBar()

The salsaBar class represents a status bar for displaying salsa bottle fill levels in the game, extending the drawableObject class.

Constructor

new salsaBar()

Constructs a new salsaBar instance. Loads the images for different fill levels, sets the position and dimensions of the salsa bar, and initializes it with a percentage of 0.
